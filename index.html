Zero-Stress Cooking Guides

Kitchen Essentials

You don't need a gourmet kitchen to cook great meals. Here's our list of must-have tools for beginners:

  • 1 good chef's knife
  • Cutting board
  • 2-3 pots/pans
  • Measuring cups/spoons
  • Mixing bowls

With just these basics, you can make 90% of the recipes on our site!

Budget Cooking

Eating well doesn't have to be expensive. Our top tips for saving money while cooking delicious meals:

  • Buy in bulk when possible
  • Learn to love eggs, beans, and rice
  • Shop seasonal produce
  • Repurpose leftovers creatively
  • Master a few staple recipes

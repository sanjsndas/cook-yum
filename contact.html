Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own recipe to CookYum?

We love receiving recipe submissions from our community! You can email your recipe to submissions@cookyum.com with the subject line "Recipe Submission". Please include a list of ingredients, step-by-step instructions, preparation time, and any photos you'd like to share. Our editorial team will review your submission and get back to you within 2-3 weeks.

How long does it take to receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during our normal business hours. Technical support requests may take up to 72 hours for a complete response. If you haven't heard back from us within this timeframe, please check your spam folder or contact us again.

Do you offer advertising or sponsorship opportunities?

Yes! We work with brands that align with our mission of making cooking quick, easy, and delicious. For advertising inquiries, please email partnerships@cookyum.com with details about your brand and what type of collaboration you're interested in. Our partnership team will get back to you with available opportunities.

Can I use your recipes in my cookbook/blog/restaurant menu?

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we're often happy to grant permission for non-commercial use with proper attribution. For commercial use, please contact us at legal@cookyum.com with details about how you'd like to use the recipe. We typically respond to these requests within 5-7 business days.

Do you have an affiliate program?

Yes, we have an affiliate program for content creators and bloggers. You can join our program through our affiliate network partner. Please email affiliates@cookyum.com for more information about commission rates, requirements, and how to apply.
